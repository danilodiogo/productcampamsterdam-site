Colour

Brand red #AA3D2F and brand green #8EAB30 are display-only on body text — they fail AAA at 5.5:1 and 3.1:1. We use #7C2A1F (red, 9:1) and #4D6118 (green, 7.5:1) for any text under 24 px.

Motion choreography

All motion respects prefers-reduced-motion: reduce. With reduce on, every animation collapses to a 200 ms opacity fade — no translate, scale, or rotate.

Section reveal

Fade-up 24 px, 600 ms cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1), triggered at 25 % in viewport. Children stagger 80 ms.

Accessibility annotations

  • Skip-to-content link is the first focusable element on every page.
  • One <h1> per page; heading levels never skip.
  • Every <img> has alt (decorative ones use alt="" and aria-hidden="true").
  • All interactive elements ≥ 44 × 44 px touch target.
  • :focus-visible always shows a 3 px #7C2A1F ring with 2 px offset.
  • prefers-reduced-motion collapses every animation to opacity-only.
  • Layout intact at 200 % zoom and at 320 px viewport width.
  • Site readable and navigable with JavaScript disabled.
