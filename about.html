About ProductCamp

Unleash your ideas.

ProductCamp Amsterdam is the first and only unconference for product managers in the Netherlands — a platform for professionals to share experiences and learn from one another.

Network

Building a community for knowledge exchange.

For more than a decade, ProductCamp has been a meeting point for senior product managers, designers, and tech leads from across the Netherlands. The day is a chance to step out of your team's bubble — and into a room of practitioners who've solved the problems you're working on.

Empowerment

Unlock your potential at ProductCamp Amsterdam.

No keynote-only auditoriums. No pre-set agenda you can't influence. The morning is yours — pitch a session, propose a topic, vote on what runs. The afternoon is where the real conversations happen, in rooms small enough to actually hear each other.
